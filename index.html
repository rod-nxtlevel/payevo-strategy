Top Friction Points
Critical

Post-Setup Email Verification

Users complete 3 registration steps and extensive company setup, then discover they must verify email and re-login manually. All momentum lost.

Critical

Forced Re-Login

After profile creation, users are sent to a "Back to Login" screen instead of being auto-redirected. Session continuity is completely broken.

Critical

Open-World Dashboard

After onboarding, users land in a complex dashboard with 15+ sidebar items, no hierarchy, and no guided path to their first payroll run.

Critical

5-Field Signup

Registration requires name, email, phone, password, and confirm password. No social sign-in. 60% more fields than necessary.

Critical

Universal CRA Fields

CRA Payroll Number and Quebec Payroll Number shown to ALL users regardless of province. No conditional logic, no help text.

Critical

Nested Payroll Setup

Company Rules, Provincial, Vacation, Deductions, Benefits, and Earnings spread across multiple nested screens requiring individual navigation.
