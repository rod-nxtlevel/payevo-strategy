Returning client

Fast lane for an existing verified relationship

If a user is opening a new entity but has a recent verified history with PayEvo, the product should not automatically treat them like a completely new applicant.

What we already know

Existing account `PE-42181`, signer previously verified 90 days ago, same primary contact.

What still needs confirmation

New legal entity, new business registration, and whether the same bank or owners still apply.

Why this matters

Good repeat clients should not be forced through every duplicate step if recent risk history is acceptable.

Reduced-step example

1
Linked account confirmed Prior relationship found and pulled into the risk view.
2
Signer reused Identity verification is still considered fresh enough for a lighter-touch review.
3
Only new-entity facts requested Confirm company details, bank selection, and any changes to ownership or risk profile.

Possible extra items
